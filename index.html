About

        Re/tell is a re-imagining or telling the story/ events through a different perspective that may not be true to the roots of realms. To be specific, the scenario can be based on either a build's feature, myths, monster, or anything realm related. Even the mechanics can be altered depending on the designated genre. Also the /tell itself is play on words with the chat command scheme. Mostly the format of the tale consist on the structure sometimes similar to a interactive fiction or should I say comic style. If you happen to dislike this sort of direction, then I suggest you to look elsewhere if it doesn't intrigued your interest.

      Info:

        Character Sprites provided by SlugKing
        Cast: Archer (You, the player who navigates), Yorick's Skull (For temporary company), Apparition
        Genre: Maze

      Rules/ Specifics:

        To navigate, press the button that's layout for you.
        There are 3 warps, it's purpose is to hinder your progress.
        2 checkpoints provided although if you get warp back and died you'll only remain in that sector until you pass the checkpoint.
        Of course there's an exit waiting for you.
